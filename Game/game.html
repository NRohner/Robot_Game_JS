Pick & Place

By Nick Rohner

This is Clint. He is a robot. You can control Clint's arms using the left and right arrow keys.

Use the spacebar to pick up green packages. To do this, you will need to position Clint's grabber close to the top of the package.

Press spacebar again to release the package. The goal is to place each package on top of its red drop zone.

Try to pick and place as many packages as you can!
